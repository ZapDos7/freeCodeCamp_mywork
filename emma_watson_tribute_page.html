Emma Watson

The feminist, the actress, the (role) model

Emma Watson, in the pondium, explaining why gender equality involves men as well as women.

Her life - Acting

  • 1990 - Born in Paris, France
  • until 2003 - Watson attended the Dragon School and trained as an actress at the Oxford branch of Stagecoach Theatre Arts.
  • 2001-2011 - Portrayed Hermione Granger in all 8 movies of the Harry Potter saga.
  • 2007 - Appeared in the television adaptation of the novel Ballet Shoes
  • 2008 - Lend her voice to The Tale of Despereaux
  • 2011 - Played in My Week with Marilyn
  • 2012 - Played in The Perks of Being a Wallflower
  • 2013 - Played in The Bling Ring, and in This is the End
  • 2014 - Portrayed the title character's adopted daughter in Noah
  • 2015 - Played in Regression and Colonia
  • 2017 - Played in The Circle, and starred in the live-action adaptation of the musical romantic fantasy film Beauty and the Beast as Belle.

Her Life - Achievements

  • From 2011 to 2014, Watson split her time between working on film projects and continuing her education, studying at Brown University and Worcester College, Oxford and graduating from Brown with a bachelor's degree in English literature in May 2014.
  • Her modelling work has included campaigns for Burberry and Lancôme. As a fashion consultant, she helped create a line of clothing for People Tree.
  • She was honoured by the British Academy of Film and Television Arts in 2014, winning for British Artist of the Year.
  • That same year, she was appointed as a UN Women Goodwill ambassador and helped launch the UN Women campaign HeForShe, which calls for men to advocate gender equality.
